Individual approval is intentional — manual review required
By Design
Facilitators and admins manually verify each student's section, name, and email before approving. A bulk-approve button would bypass this check and risk letting incorrectly registered students through. One-by-one approval is the correct workflow for this department.
dashboard/admin/users/page.tsx — no change needed
Ghost accounts on two-step registration failure
High
Registration is two steps: (1) supabase.auth.signUp() → creates the auth user, (2) POST /api/auth/register → creates the profile. If step 2 fails (network error, rate limit, DB error), the student has a ghost auth user — can't register again (email taken), can't log in (no profile). There is no auth-user cleanup in registerStudent().
